About Me

Jacques grew up in State College, Pennsylvania. After graduating from Penn State in 2012 with a BSc in Petroleum and Natural Gas Engineering, He then began work in Houston as a completions engineer in the oil and gas industry. After a few years, he took a break and moved to Colorado, working as a ski instructor at Breckenridge.

Currently, Jacques is learning a new trade, programming. This is his first website. Please wish him good fortune on this rigorous journey.
